Frequently Asked Questions

How are service providers verified? +
All our service providers undergo thorough background checks, document verification, and training. They are also rated by other residents.
What if I'm not satisfied with the service? +
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the service, we'll send another professional.
Are the prices fixed or negotiable? +
Resireach Platform does not charge any fees. Prices are set by the service providers based on the specific service and are generally fixed. However, you can discuss any specific requirements directly with them.
How do I make payments? +
You can pay directly to the service provider based on your services.
