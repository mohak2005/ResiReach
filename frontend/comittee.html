Roles & Responsibilities

Our RWA committee members have specific responsibilities to ensure smooth functioning of our community.

President

Overall administration, presiding meetings, representing society in legal matters.

Secretary

Day-to-day operations, maintaining records, correspondence, organizing meetings.

Treasurer

Financial management, budgeting, collection of maintenance charges, accounting.

Executive Members

Supporting core functions, specific portfolio assignments, grievance redressal.
