Our Story

Our Journey

2005

Concept & Planning

Initial concept development and planning for a premium residential community began.

2007

Construction Begins

Groundbreaking ceremony and commencement of construction on the 10-acre property.

2008

First Residents Move In

Phase 1 completed and the first families moved into Skyhills Apartment.

2010

Community Center Inauguration

State-of-the-art community center with swimming pool and gym facilities opened.

2015

Green Initiatives Launch

Implemented rainwater harvesting and solar power systems, becoming an eco-friendly community.

2020

Smart Community Upgrade

Implemented smart security systems and high-speed fiber internet throughout the complex.
